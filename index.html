[Player Interaction]

Manage the Network

In Signal Foundry, players don’t just solve tasks — they build and manage a living network. It’s important to watch how energy flows, identify weak points, and optimize the system.

Players can:

  • connect and reroute signals to ensure energy flows correctly,

  • balance loads between nodes and prevent overloads,

  • use stabilizers to smooth out signal flow.
